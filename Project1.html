LEDS, CAMERA, ACTION!
Soldering

The first part of this project is to solder the wiring and board together to create a whole circuit. The images below showcase the soldering job done to get the electronics to start working.

The next step in creating my circuit was creating a switch. I created a switch using a clothing pin and cut groves into it to make space for a steel wire for a solid connection. I then tied wires to the steel wires instead of soldering because it was galvanized steel.

The wiring is a bit messy but this show cased how i wired and guided all the wires together.

Artist Statement

The Proscenium I made is more akin to a venue that plays concerts and raves. It is a small hobby to attend these events for the loud music and the visuals and crowd. It is a lot of a fun and what is representative of my stage is definetly meant to capture that whimsicleness. I believe I was able to capture a simple yet effective way to portray my ideas. The cardboard housing with the cardboard crowd along with realisitc elements that takes up stage is in telling interesting to see.
